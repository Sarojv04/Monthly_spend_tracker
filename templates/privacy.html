Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address at registration
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter manually
  • Usage data — basic session information to keep you logged in securely

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is needed to run the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within your account
  • To authenticate you securely when you log in
  • To improve the app's features and performance over time

We never use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the core tracking experience.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a local database associated with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, including password hashing and secure session handling. However, no system is completely immune to risk, so please use a strong, unique password for your account.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly loads fonts from Google Fonts to render the interface correctly. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Beyond this, we do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, or if you would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us at:

  • Email: sarojv04july@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.

This policy applies to the current version of Spendly. As the app evolves, this page will be updated to reflect any changes in how data is collected or used.
